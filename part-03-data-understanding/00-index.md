# Part III: Data Understanding

Before you can train a model, you need to understand what you have. This part builds the EDA vocabulary and habits that let you assess any dataset's structure, quality, and distributions — corresponding to the CRISP-DM Data Understanding phase. The skills you develop here are prerequisites for every modeling task that follows.
